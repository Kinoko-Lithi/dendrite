Statement

“Tasos Zisis is a digital artist and musician. His work focuses on photographic collage and video art, exploring immersive and multi-layered topographies through digital image alteration, sound composition, and texture-based techniques. With an academic background in biological anthropology, music, and history, he creates audiovisual narrations influenced by human anatomy and the environment.”
